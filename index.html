Welcome to Jenergy Electrical and Solar

Everyone has different reasons for going solar, at Jenergy we are dedicated to fighting climate change and saving you money while we're at it. It's the best of both worlds.

With a decade of experience in the solar industry, our experienced team of qualified electricians specialize in domestic and commercial installations. From standard houses to custom designer homes, we've seen it all, and we've got you covered.

How it works

  • Step 1: Book in a consultation with one of our solar professionals. We will discuss budget, your electricity generation requirements, rebates, and go through all and any questions that you may have.
  • Step 2: Based on the information discussed, we will draw up a suitable solar panel plan for your property.
  • Step 3: Once a proposal is signed, we will proceed to contact your energy provider for permission to get started. This process is called 'pre-approval'. For any Solar Victoria rebates, you will receive a form to fill out. Fill it out and you'll be able to receive the government funding.
  • Step 4: We will schedule a suitable date and time for the installation.
  • Step 5: Once your solar installation is completed, we will organize an inspector from EnergySafe Victoria to check that everything is safe and working as it should be. He will issue a compliance certificate and then you can start harnessing energy from the sun to power your home.
